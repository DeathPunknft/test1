AD Time Value Calculation Handbook

Introduction

Welcome to the AD Time Value CalculationHandbook for digital billboards. This guide provides a step-by-step process for determining the Time Efficiency Power Value (formerly Rarity Value) and AD TimeValue (formerly Proportion Value) of each zone on a digital billboard. The calculations aim to allocate the AD Time Value among zones based on their performance.

The Time Efficiency Power Value is a measure of the relative importance of each zone on a digital billboard, indicating its uniqueness and desirability. The AD Time Value is used to distribute the AD Time Value among zones, ensuring a fair and equitable distribution of rewards and incentives.

AD Time Value Acquisition Price and AIPV Calculation

AD companies calculate the acquisition price for acquiring future one-month AD time value based on several factors:

1. Past operational performance and historical data analysis— utilizing past revenue records for statistical analysis.

2. Estimation of possible events in the upcoming month (e.g., holidays).

3. Average operational performance after a one-year actuarial calculation.

Subsequently, through the AI Profit Model(AIPM), an AI-driven profit calculation model, the calculated result is the acquisition price, also known as AIPV (AI Profit Value).

Acquisition Price is location-specific and independent of other locations. Consider the following examples for three locations:

- Location A: Acquisition Price = $10,000

- Location B: Acquisition Price = $5,000

- Location C: Acquisition Price = $2,000

Total Acquisition Price Calculation

To determine the total Acquisition Price generated from all locations, add up the Acquisition Price from each location:

Total Acquisition Price = Acquisition Price of Location A + Acquisition Price of Location B + Acquisition Price of LocationC

Example: Total Acquisition Price = $10,000+ $5,000 + $2,000 = $17,000

AD Time Value Calculation by Location

The AD Time Value is used to distribute theAD Time Value among zones on a digital billboard, allocating each zone 's share of the Total Acquisition Price based on their contribution. Calculate the ADTime Value for each location using the following formula:

Location 's AD Time Value = (Acquisition value of Location / Total Acquisition value) * 100

Example: Location A 's Total AD Time Value =($10,000 / $17,000) * 100 = 58.82

Location B 's Total AD Time Value = ($5,000/ $17,000) * 100 = 29.41

Location C 's Total AD Time Value = ($2,000/ $17,000) * 100 = 11.76

Time Efficiency Power Value (FormerlyRarity Value)

Each zone on a digital billboard is assigned a Time Efficiency Power Value, representing its uniqueness and desirability.These values were formerly known as Rarity Values. Time Efficiency Power Values are predefined and fixed. Zones closer to the billboard 's center are given higher Time Efficiency Power Values, indicating their greater value and demand.

Digital billboards typically consist of five zones, ranging from Zone 1 to Zone 5. Zone 1 has a Time Efficiency PowerValue of 3, Zone 2 is 1.5, Zone 3 is 1, Zone 4 is 0.6, and Zone 5 is 0.36.

Time Efficiency Power Value Calculation by Zone

To calculate the Time Efficiency PowerValue for each zone within a location, use the zone 's Time Efficiency PowerValue and the total AD Time Value of the location. Apply the formula as follows:

Zone’s AD Time Value of location = (TimeEfficiency Power Value / Sum of All Time Efficiency Power Values in Location) *Total AD Time Value of Location

Example: Zone 1 's AD Time Value forLocation A = (Time Efficiency Power Value 3 / (3 + 1.5 + 1 + 0.6 + 0.3)) *58.88 = 27.6

Repeat this calculation for each zonewithin the location to determine their respective AD Time Values.

Value of Acquisition for RWA Holders

Once the AD Time Value for each RWA iscalculated, every RWA holder will be associated with a specific AD Time Value.The total AD Time Value for RWA holders is computed by aggregating the AD TimeValues of all RWAs held in each holder 's wallet. This cumulative value is usedto proportionally distribute earnings, ensuring a fair distribution of the acquisition amount based on each RWA holder 's contribution.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the AD Time ValueCalculation Handbook provides a comprehensive framework for evaluating the TimeEfficiency Power Value and AD Time Value of digital billboard zones. By incorporating past operational performance, future estimations, and leveraging the AI Profit Model (AIPM), this handbook facilitates the determination of an acquisition price, or AIPV (AI Profit Value), for AD time value.

The calculated AD Time Value is then judiciously distributed among digital billboard zones, taking into account their respective contributions. This ensures a fair and equitable allocation of rewards and incentives across locations, aligning with each zone 's uniqueness and desirability as measured by the Time Efficiency Power Value.

For RWA holders, this handbook serves as a guide to understanding the value of their acquisitions. The AD Time Value foreach RWA is quantified, and the cumulative AD Time Value for all RWA holders is utilized to distribute earnings proportionally. This methodology under scores our commitment to fairness and transparency in the digital billboard ecosystem, where rewards reflect the individual contributions of RWA holders.

广告时间价值计算手册

介绍

欢迎使用广告时间价值计算手册，适用于数字广告牌。本指南提供了一个逐步的过程，用于确定数字广告牌上每个区域的时间效率动力值（以前称为稀有度值）和广告时间值（以前称为比例值）。这些计算旨在根据它们的表现分配广告时间值给不同的区域。

时间效率动力值是数字广告牌上每个区域相对重要性的度量，表明它的独特性和吸引力。广告时间值用于在区域之间分配广告时间值，确保奖励和激励的公平和公正分配。

广告时间值获取价格和AIPV计算

广告公司根据以下几个因素计算未来一个月广告时间值的获取价格：

1. 过去的运营表现和历史数据分析——利用过去的收入记录进行统计分析。

2. 对未来一个月可能发生事件的估计（例如，假期）。

3. 一年精算计算后的平均运营表现。

随后，通过AI利润模型（AIPM），一种人工智能驱动的利润计算模型，计算出的结果是获取价格，也称为AIPV（AI利润值）。

获取价格是特定于位置的，独立于其他位置。以下是三个位置的示例：

- A位置：获取价格=$10,000

- B位置：获取价格=$5,000

- C位置：获取价格=$2,000

总获取价格计算

要确定从所有位置生成的总获取价格，请将每个位置的获取价格相加：

总获取价格= A位置获取价格+ B位置获取价格+ C位置获取价格

示例：总获取价格=$10,000 + $5,000 + $2,000 = $17,000

按位置计算广告时间值

广告时间值用于在数字广告牌上的区域之间分配广告时间值，根据它们的贡献分配每个区域在总获取价格中的份额。使用以下公式为每个位置计算广告时间值：

位置的广告时间值=（位置的获取值/总获取值）* 100

示例：A位置的总广告时间值=（$10,000 /$17,000）* 100 =58.82

B位置的总广告时间值=（$5,000 /$17,000）* 100 =29.41

C位置的总广告时间值=（$2,000 /$17,000）* 100 =11.76

时效力值（稀有度值）

数字广告牌上的每个区域都被分配一个时间效率动力值，代表其独特性和吸引力。这些值以前被称为稀有度值。时间效率动力值是预定义且固定的。离广告牌中心越近的区域具有较高的时间效率动力值，表明它们的价值和需求更大。

数字广告牌通常包含五个区域，从区域1到区域5。区域1的时间效率动力值为3，区域2为1.5，区域3为1，区域4为0.6，区域5为0.3。

按区域计算时效力值

要计算位置内每个区域的时效力值，请使用区域的时间效率动力值和位置的总广告时间值。应用以下公式：

区域的广告时间值=（时间效率动力值/位置中所有时间效率动力值的总和）* 位置的总广告时间值

示例：A位置的区域1的广告时间值=（时间效率动力值3 /（3 + 1.5 + 1+ 0.6 + 0.3））* 58.88 =27.6

对于位置内的每个区域，请重复此计算以确定它们各自的广告时间值。

RWA持有者的获取价值

计算每个RWA的广告时间值后，每个RWA持有者将与特定的广告时间值相关联。 RWA持有者的总广告时间值是通过汇总每个持有者钱包中持有的所有RWA的广告时间值来计算的。此累积值用于按比例分配收入，确保根据每个RWA持有者的贡献公平分配获取金额。

结论

总之，广告时间值计算手册提供了一个评估数字广告牌区域的时间效率动力值和广告时间值的全面框架。通过整合过去的运营表现，未来的估算，并利用AI利润模型（AIPM），本手册促进了对广告时间值的获取价格或AIPV（AI利润值）的确定。

然后，计算出的广告时间值被谨慎地分配给数字广告牌区域，考虑到它们各自的贡献。这确保了在不同位置之间公平而公正地分配奖励和激励，与每个区域的独特性和吸引力一致，如时间效率动力值所衡量的。

对于RWA持有者，本手册是了解其获取价值的指南。每个RWA的广告时间值都是量化的，所有RWA持有者的累积广告时间值用于按比例分配收入。这种方法强调了我们在数字广告牌生态系统中致力于公平和透明的承诺，其中奖励反映了RWA持有者个体贡献的原则。
