Introduction to RWA AD Time Value

Overview

Welcome to the world of RWA (Real WorldAsset) advertising on digital billboards. This document aims to provide you with a clear understanding of your RWA AD Time Value, the Acquisition Price, and the exciting options available to you.

RWA AD Time Value

As an RWA holder, you possess the AD TimeValue of digital billboards. It 's essential to note that this ownership pertains to the potential earnings from the digital billboard, not the physical billboard itself. Your AD Time Value represents the value that can be generated over time.

**Note:** The AD Time Value has a 2-year expiration. If no decisions are made in the marketplace within this period, the accumulated value will be canceled.

Acquisition Price

The Acquisition Price is the value assigned to your AD Time Value, presented in USDT (Tether) or eHKD. This is the amount advertising companies are willing to pay to acquire the rights to use your ADTime Value. The Acquisition Price is calculated based on various factors, including market conditions, past operational performance, and predictions for upcoming events such as holidays.

Your Options

1. Sell AD Time Value

Description: In this option, you choose to sell your AD Time Value to advertising companies in exchange for theAcquisition Price.

Process: You essentially trade one month of your advertising time, transferring ownership of the AD Time Value to the advertising company for that period.

2. Top-Up Card Option (Coming Soon)

Description: This exciting option allows you to charge the Acquisition Price to a shopping top-up card.

Benefits: Once launched, the shopping top-up card can be used for various purchases, offering discounts at restaurants, shopping malls, supermarkets, and more. Opting for this option provides an additional 10% value on the Acquisition Price.

**Note:** The Top-Up Card option is not yet launched, and you can 't choose it at this moment. Stay tuned for updates.

Conclusion

As an RWA holder, you have the option to sell and relinquish ownership rights to the time value, enabling digital advertising board owners to purchase this valuable advertising time.Understanding this dynamic relationship ensures a mutually beneficial ecosystem where both RWA holders and digital advertising board owners can thrive. Having a clear understanding of your RWA AD Value and the corresponding AcquisitionPrice empowers you to make well-informed decisions. Whether you choose to sell your AD Time Value directly or opt for the upcoming Top-Up Card option, these choices are designed to enhance your experience as an RWA holder.

RWA广告时间价值介绍

概览

欢迎来到数字广告牌上RWA（真实世界资产）广告的世界。本文旨在为您提供关于RWA广告时间价值、收购价格以及可用于您的令人兴奋的选择的清晰了解。

RWA广告时间价值

作为RWA持有者，您拥有数字广告牌的广告时间价值。需要注意的是，这种所有权涉及的是来自数字广告牌的潜在收入，而不是物理广告牌本身。您的广告时间价值代表了可以随时间生成的价值。

**注意：** 广告时间价值有效期为2年。如果在此期间未在市场上做出任何决定，累积值将被取消。

收购价格

收购价格是分配给您广告时间价值的价值，以USDT（泰达币） 或 eHKD呈现。这是广告公司愿意支付的金额，以获取使用您的广告时间价值的权利。收购价格的计算基于各种因素，包括市场条件、过去的运营绩效以及对即将发生的事件（如假期）的预测。

您的选择

1. 出售广告时间价值

描述： 在此选项中，您选择将您的广告时间价值出售给广告公司，以换取收购价格。

过程： 您基本上交易了您广告时间的一个月，将广告时间价值的所有权转让给广告公司。

2. 储值卡选项（即将推出）

描述： 这个令人兴奋的选项允许您将收购价格充值到购物储值卡中。

优势： 一旦推出，购物储值卡可用于各种购物，提供在餐馆、购物中心、超市等地享受折扣的机会。选择此选项将为收购价格提供额外的10%价值。

**注意：** 储值卡选项目前尚未推出，您目前无法选择。敬请期待更新。

结论

作为RWA持有者，您可以选择出售并放弃广告时间的所有权，从而使数字广告牌所有者购买这一宝贵的广告时间。了解这种动态关系确保了一个相互有益的生态系统，在这个生态系统中，RWA持有者和数字广告牌所有者都能够蓬勃发展。清晰了解您的RWA广告价值及其相应的收购价格，使您能够做出明智的决策。无论您选择直接出售广告时间价值还是选择即将推出的顶级卡选项，这些选择都旨在提升您作为RWA持有者的体验。
